About OJTify

Built to solve the real pain of OJT management

OJTify was built specifically for OJT programs — eliminating the manual, paper-based chaos of traditional internship tracking. Students lose logbooks. Teachers chase signatures. Admins spend hours generating reports. OJTify solves all of that digitally, in one place.

Our partner school is Olongapo City Elementary School (OCES), where OJTify is deployed to modernize how student teachers manage their internship hours and documentation.

  • No more paper logbooks or manual DTR sheets
  • Real-time progress monitoring for admins
  • Instant QR-based attendance — no hardware needed
  • Auto-generate DTR & weekly reports, ready to print
  • Digital submission directly to your supervisor or school
The Problem

Traditional OJT tracking relies on physical logbooks, manual attendance sheets, and slow reporting — leading to errors, lost documents, and wasted time.

The OJTify Solution

OJTify digitizes the entire OJT process — from QR attendance to printable DTR and auto-generated weekly reports — making internship management effortless.

Partner School

Olongapo City Elementary School (OCES) — our pilot partner school where OJTify is actively deployed and used by student teachers and cooperating teachers.

Features

Everything you need for OJT

Powerful tools built for every role in the internship process.

QR Code Attendance

Students scan a daily QR code to clock in and out instantly. No hardware, no manual sheets — just a smartphone.

Internship Dashboard

A personalized hub showing real-time hours rendered, attendance rate, weekly progress, and pending submissions.

Digital Logbook

Daily task logging with cooperating teacher review and approval — all stored securely in the cloud.

Supervisor Evaluation

Structured evaluation forms for cooperating teachers to rate intern performance on competencies and behavior.

DTR & Report Generation

Auto-generate Daily Time Records (DTR) and weekly reports with one click — print-ready and formatted for school submission.

Supervisor Submission

Submit finalized reports and DTRs directly to your cooperating teacher or supervisor through the platform — streamlined and secure.

Who Uses OJTify?

Built for every role in the OJT process

Intern / OJT Student

Track your OJT journey from day one to completion — all from your phone or laptop.

  • QR attendance scanning (time-in/out)
  • Daily logbook entry submission
  • View supervisor evaluations
  • Generate & print DTR
  • Submit weekly reports to supervisor

Supervisor / Cooperating Teacher

Evaluate and monitor your student teachers without extra paperwork.

  • View student attendance records
  • Approve or reject logbook entries
  • Submit performance evaluations
  • Track intern progress in real time
  • Communicate with school admin

Admin / School Coordinator

Full control over the system — manage users, monitor everything, and ensure smooth OJT operations.

  • Manage all student & supervisor accounts
  • Review & approve student applications
  • Monitor system-wide OJT progress
  • Oversee submissions and approvals
  • Configure system settings & QR codes
